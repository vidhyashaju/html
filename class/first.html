TheUnited Arab Emirates (UAE; Arabic: اَلْإِمَارَات الْعَرَبِيَة الْمُتَحِدَة al-ʾImārāt al-ʿArabīyah al-Muttaḥidah),
or simply the Emirates (Arabic: الِْإمَارَات al-ʾImārāt), is a country in Western Asia (the Middle East).
It is located at the eastern end of the Arabian Peninsula and shares borders border with Oman and Saudi Arabia, while having maritime borders in the Persian Gulf with Qatar and Iran. Abu Dhabi is the nation's capital, while Dubai, the most populated city, is an international hub.
